Clans/Groups:

ThunderClan: They are courageous and honorable. They are skilled hunters and can be bossy at times. Fun Fact: There has been at least one ThunderClan protagonist every series.

RiverClan: RiverClan cats are skillful and graceful. They are known for being great swimmers and live by the river. They eat fish. Fun Fact: Back in the forest territories, they decorated their dens with shiny Twoleg things.

WindClan: This Clan is swift and hardy. They are great runners and live on the moor. Their main prey is rabbits, and they used to be split between moor runners and tunnelers. Fun Fact: Runningbrook, Tornear's apprentice, simply disappeared without even one line.

ShadowClan: These cats are fierce and proud. They are secretive and hunt in the dark. Their territory is the smallest, but they have made up for it. Fun Fact: ShadowClan hasn't had a known female leader since Shadowstar.

SkyClan: This Clan has cats who are agile and creative. They climb trees and are the newest Clan to the lake, and as a result have many new ideas such as the invention of mediators. Fun Fact: Cloudstar's kits, Spottedpelt and Gorseclaw, carry SkyClan blood into ThunderClan, ShadowClan, and RiverClan.

BloodClan: BloodClan is a Clan formed by Scourge. Most cats are either bloodthristy or go along with the leader's orders. They are a group of street cats that live in Twolegplace. Fun Fact: Barley was orginally a BloodClan cat.
